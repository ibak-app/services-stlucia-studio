What is the SLU Services Directory?

The SLU Services Directory is a curated, searchable database of service providers across Saint Lucia. We aggregate listings from Facebook groups, WhatsApp broadcasts, local advertising, and direct outreach â€” then organise them by category and district and publish them here for free.

Every listing includes a direct WhatsApp link with a pre-written introduction message, so you can connect with a provider in one tap. No account needed. No middleman. No commission.

We cover 40+ service categories: from home repairs and automotive to beauty, food, events, tech support, education, and more. New listings are added daily.

How We Build the Directory

Our team monitors Facebook marketplace listings, community groups, WhatsApp broadcasts, and local classifieds across Saint Lucia. When we find a service provider, we:

  • Record their WhatsApp number and contact details
  • Confirm the service area and district
  • Record available pricing and hours information
  • Assign to the correct service category
  • Publish the listing to the directory

If you find incorrect information, please report it â€” we review all reports within 48 hours.
